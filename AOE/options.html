Quick Guide

  • Open WebUI URL: This is the address you use to access Open WebUI in your browser. You can also append ?model=your-model to set a default model.
  • Target Match URL: This is required for the extension to communicate with your Open WebUI instance. It must end with /* to match all sub-paths.
